# Outcome Works: Brand Style Guide

The canonical reference for the Outcome Works identity. Every value here is the
live source of truth, pulled from the website design system
(`src/styles/global.css`) and brand assets (`public/`). Use it to build a formal
brand guide, brief designers, or keep collateral on-brand.

---

## 1. Brand essence

| | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Outcome Works Limited |
| **Tagline** | Built to win the work that's hard to win. |
| **What we do** | AI advisory, bid & proposal support, and the OWL software suite, for teams competing in public-sector and enterprise procurement. |
| **Organising idea** | **One brand, two doors.** Door A = Consultancy (work with our team). Door B = Products (run the OWL suite). |
| **Audience** | Risk-averse senior buyers in public-sector and enterprise procurement. They research quietly before making contact. |
| **Personality** | Trust-first, understated, evidence-led, number-first. Senior and precise. We never oversell. |
| **The owl** | The logo is an owl: navy body, cyan wing, two large white eyes. **Two eyes = two doors** is the brand's signature conceptual hook. The product suite is itself named OWL, so the master brand and the products share one mark and one name. |

## 2. Logo

### The mark
A navy owl with a teal/cyan wing and two large white eyes. Available as
`public/owl.png` (425 × 521, transparent background).

### Lockup
Owl mark + wordmark "**Outcome Works**" + "**Limited**" (mono, wide-tracked).
- Wordmark: Space Grotesk, bold, tight tracking.
- "Limited": IBM Plex Mono, 10px, letter-spacing 0.14em, muted/40%-white.
- Owl-to-wordmark gap: ~10px. Owl aspect ratio 0.82 (w/h).

### Sizing
- Navbar mark: **28px** owl tall. Footer mark: **32px** owl tall.
- Minimum legible mark: ~16px (favicon). Below that, use the eyes-only read.

### Variants
- **On light** (paper/white): full-colour owl, ink wordmark.
- **On dark** (navy/ink): full-colour owl (white eyes pop), white wordmark.
- **Mono watermark**: single white tone via `filter: brightness(0) invert(1)`
  (`.owl-mono`), used faint (~5% opacity) on dark grounds. A true mono SVG is the
  clean upgrade when available.

### Clear space & don'ts
- Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of one owl eye.
- Do not recolour the owl, stretch it, add effects, or place the full-colour
  navy owl on a dark ground (use the mono treatment instead).

### Derived assets (in `public/`, regenerated by `scripts/gen-brand-assets.mjs`)
`favicon.svg` (owl embedded), `favicon.ico`, `favicon-16.png`, `favicon-32.png`,
`apple-touch-icon.png` (owl on paper, rounded), `og-image.png` (1200 × 630 social
card: owl on navy + cyan glow).

### Eye-pair motif
A twin-circle glyph (one teal ring, one terracotta ring) abstracts the owl's eyes
into the two-door system. Use sparingly, once per surface, as a quiet brand stamp.

### Colour rules
- **Master pair is navy + cyan.** Use **teal** for consultancy-themed content and
  **terracotta** for product-themed content. Otherwise stay navy / cyan / neutral.
- **One accent family per section** (Colour Consistency Lock). Do not introduce a
  second accent mid-section.
- **No AI-purple, no neon glows, no pure black.** Off-black (`#14202B`) only.
- Theme is **locked light**. Dark bands (footer, dual-CTA, mesh grounds) are
  deliberate navy blocks, not a theme flip.

## 4. Typography

### Typefaces
- **Space Grotesk** (variable): display, headings, and body. Self-hosted.
- **IBM Plex Mono** (400, 500): labels, kickers, captions, data, page numbers.
- **Fallbacks** for environments without the brand fonts (e.g. Word): body/headings
  → Aptos or Calibri; mono → Consolas. CSS stack: `'Space Grotesk Variable',
  system-ui, sans-serif` and `'IBM Plex Mono', ui-monospace, 'SF Mono', monospace`.

### Type scale (web, responsive `clamp`)
| Element | Size | Line height | Tracking | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | `clamp(40px, 5.4vw, 64px)` | 1.04 | -0.022em | 700 |
| H2 | `clamp(28px, 3.8vw, 44px)` | 1.1 | -0.016em | 700 |
| H3 | `clamp(19px, 2.4vw, 23px)` | 1.25 | tight | 600 |
| Body | 16.5px | 1.6 | normal | 400 |
| Lead (`.lede`) | `clamp(17px, 1.8vw, 20px)` | relaxed | normal | 400, muted, max 56ch |
| Kicker (`.kicker`) | 11.5px | n/a | 0.14em, UPPERCASE | 500, mono, muted |

### Type rules
- Headings are **navy or ink**, bold, tightly tracked, `text-wrap: balance`.
- Body sits at a comfortable measure (~65ch max). Lead paragraphs cap at 56ch.
- Mono is **rationed**: genuine labels, data, and captions only. Not decorative.
- Emphasis within a heading uses bold/italic of the **same** family, never a second font.

## 5. Voice & tone

- **Trust-first and number-first.** Lead with the quantified outcome
  (e.g. "3x tender throughput"), then the story.
- **No overselling.** The number carries the weight.
- **Banned hype words:** revolutionise, seamless, unleash, next-gen, elevate,
  cutting-edge, and similar filler. Use concrete verbs.
- **NDA register:** names are usually under NDA, so lead with the sector and the
  measured result ("Law enforcement client", "Sector-led, unnamed client").
- **Hard rule: no em-dash or en-dash anywhere visible.** The only permitted dash is
  the regular hyphen. Use a period, comma, colon, or parentheses instead. This is
  non-negotiable across web and collateral.
- Sentence case for most UI; concise, plain, senior.

## 6. Layout & spacing

- **Content width:** `max-w-[1080px]`, centred, with `px-5 sm:px-6` gutters.
- **Section rhythm:** vertical padding `py-16` to `py-20` (md) for standard
  sections; tighter (`py-12`) for utility bands.
- **Breakpoints:** sm 640 · md 768 · lg 1024 · xl 1280 · 2xl 1536.
- **Corner radius (Shape Lock):** cards = `rounded-lg`; buttons/inputs = `rounded-md`;
  pills/eye-rings = full. Apply consistently; do not mix systems.
- **Grids over flex-math.** Multi-column layouts use CSS Grid with explicit mobile
  collapse to single column.
- **Hairlines over boxes.** Separate content with `border` / `divide` in `line`
  / `hairline` colour rather than heavy containers.

## 7. Components & UI patterns

### Buttons
| Variant | Style |
|---|---|
| primary | `bg-ink` white text, hover → `bg-spine`, lifts 1px, subtle shadow |
| consult | `bg-consult` white text, hover brightness +10%, lifts 1px |
| product | `bg-product` white text, hover brightness +10%, lifts 1px |
| ghost | `border-line`, ink text, hover border → ink |
| white | white bg, ink text (for dark grounds) |
| outline-white | white/40 border, white text (for dark grounds) |

Base: `rounded-md`, 14.5px medium, `px-6 py-3`, 200ms transition. Labels ≤ 3 words,
one line, one CTA intent per page.

### Signature blocks
- **DoorCard**: the two-door fork. Teal-wash (consult) or terracotta-wash (product)
  card with eyebrow, title, body, and an arrow CTA. Lifts on hover.
- **Stat / CaseMetric**: number-first proof. Large figure in the accent colour,
  mono label beneath, sober supporting copy.
- **ProductCard**: OWL tool card. `border-t` terracotta tab, name, mono job label,
  short description, arrow.
- **TrustPill**: mono sector chip, full-radius, tinted (spine / consult / product).
- **SectionLabel**: a short tinted tick (`h-px w-6`) + mono kicker. **No section
  numbers.** Rationed to ~1 per 3 sections (hero counts as one).
- **PageHero**: inner-page hero with a tone-coloured left accent rule, kicker, H1,
  lead, optional CTA.
- **DualCTA**: closing dark band: Consultancy (teal) + Products (terracotta) cards
  on the navy mesh ground, with the eye-pair glyph and a faint owl watermark.

### Forms
- Label **above** input (mono, uppercase, 0.1em). Helper below. No placeholder-as-label.
- Inputs: white, `border-line`, `rounded-md`; focus → consult border + soft ring.
- Placeholder uses `muted` (AA), never `faint`.

### Tables (collateral)
Navy header row, white mono headers; hairline row separators (`#CDD5DC`); optional
`#ECEFF2` zebra; no vertical borders. Editorial, not a heavy grid.

## 8. Texture & graphic devices

No stock photography or fake screenshots. Visual interest comes from brand-tinted
texture:
- **`.mesh-spine`**: navy (`#06395B`) ground with a top-left cyan glow
  `rgba(23,158,178,0.24)` and a bottom-right terracotta glow `rgba(229,145,108,0.13)`.
  The standard dark band.
- **`.grid-faint`**: 56px hairline grid, radially masked to fade out. Overlaid on
  mesh grounds at low opacity.
- **`.framed`**: 11px corner brackets (top-left + bottom-right), `currentColor` at
  45% opacity. The masthead motif.
- **Owl watermark**: `.owl-mono` owl, faint, on dark grounds.

---

## 9. Motion

- **Restraint (level ~4 of 10).** Motion is trust-first, never flashy.
- **`.reveal`**: CSS-only scroll-in (fade + 20px rise) via `animation-timeline:
  view()`. No JS, no library. Content is visible by default where unsupported.
- Animate **transform and opacity only**. Honour `prefers-reduced-motion`
  (all motion collapses to static).
- Hover affordances: 1px lift / slight brightness on interactive elements.

---

## 10. Iconography
- Library: Lucide (line icons), used for nav and directional cues (arrows).
- Keep one icon family and a consistent stroke weight. Icons are functional, not decorative.

---

## 11. Accessibility
- Target **WCAG AA**: body text ≥ 4.5:1, large text ≥ 3:1. `consult`, `muted`, and
  all CTA combinations are chosen to pass.
- Visible focus: 2px `consult` outline, 2px offset.
- Theme locked light; full colour hierarchy maintained on dark bands.
